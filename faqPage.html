Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I book tickets to visit the museum?
A: You can book your tickets online through our official website. Click on the "Book online" button on the homepage, select your visit date and ticket type, and follow the prompts to complete the payment process.
Q: What facilities are available for disabled visitors?
A: The museum provides comprehensive facilities for disabled visitors, including accessible entrances, elevators, and specialized restrooms. We also offer wheelchair rental services; for more details, please inquire at the front desk.
Q: What exhibitions does the museum offer?
A: We feature a variety of permanent and temporary exhibitions covering areas such as history, art, and technology. For detailed information about our exhibitions, please visit the "Exhibitions" page on our website.
Q: Can I take photos inside the museum?
A: Photography is allowed in most exhibit areas, but please turn off your flash and be mindful not to disturb other visitors. Specific exhibitions or items may have different rules; please follow the guidance of onsite staff.
Q: What are the opening hours of the museum?
A: We are open all week from 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with the last admission at 4:00 PM.
Q: How do I get to the museum?
A: You can reach us by public transport or by car. If using public transport, take multiple bus routes to the "Museum North Gate Station". For detailed directions and parking information, please visit the "Visiting" page on our website.
